ice cream

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups heavy cream
  2. 1 cup whole milk
  3. Â¾ cup granulated sugar
  4. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  5. A pinch of salt

Instructions:

Step 1

Combine Ingredients:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.

Step 2

Chill the Mixture:
Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight. This step is crucial for achieving a smooth and creamy texture.

Step 3

Churn the Ice Cream:
Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. This usually takes about 20-30 minutes.

Step 4

Freeze:
Once the ice cream has reached a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to an airtight container. Freeze for at least 2-4 hours, or until firm.

Step 5

Serve:
Scoop and enjoy!
Tips and Variations: For a richer flavor: Use vanilla bean paste instead of vanilla extract. Add mix-ins: Once the ice cream is churned, you can fold in your favorite mix-ins, such as chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or fruit pieces.

Step 6

Fruit variations:
Mash fresh fruit, like strawberries or raspberries, with a little sugar, and swirl it into the ice cream before freezing.
Chocolate variation:
Add cocoa powder to the mixture for a chocolate ice cream. You can also melt chocolate and swirl it into the ice cream.

Caramel variation: Swirl in caramel sauce while transferring the ice cream to the container for freezing.
