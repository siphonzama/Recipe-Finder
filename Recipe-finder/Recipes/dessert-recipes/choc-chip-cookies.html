Chocolate chip cookies/h2>

Ingredients:

24 cookies

  1. 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  2. ¾ cup granulated sugar
  3. ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. 1 teaspoon salt
  9. 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2

Cream butter and sugars: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.

Step 3

Beat in eggs and vanilla: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.

Step 4

Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.

Step 5

Combine wet and dry ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

Step 6

Stir in chocolate chips: Stir in the chocolate chips.
Bake: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
Cool: Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 7

chocolate chip cookies Tips for Perfect Cookies: Softened butter: Make sure your butter is softened to room temperature for smooth creaming. Don't overmix: Overmixing the dough can lead to tough cookies.

Step 8

Chill the dough: For thicker, chewier cookies, chill the dough for at least 30 minutes before baking. Use a cookie scoop: For uniform cookies, use a cookie scoop. Don't overbake: Overbaked cookies will be dry. Cool on a rack: Let cookies cool on a wire rack to prevent them from becoming soggy.
