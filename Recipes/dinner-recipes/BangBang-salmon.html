Bang Bang Salmon with Avocado Cucumber Salsa

INGREDIENTS:

  1. 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  2. Salt and pepper, to taste
  3. 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  4. 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  5. 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
  6. 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  7. 1 tablespoon honey
  8. 1 avocado, diced
  9. 1 cucumber, diced
  10. 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  11. 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  12. Juice of 1 lime
  13. Olive oil, for cooking

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. In a shallow dish, spread the panko breadcrumbs. Press each salmon fillet into the breadcrumbs, coating them evenly on all sides. Place the coated fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the coating is golden brown and crispy.
  5. While the salmon is baking, prepare the bang bang sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sweet chili sauce, sriracha sauce, and honey until smooth. Adjust the spiciness by adding more or less sriracha according to your preference.
  6. Next, make the avocado cucumber salsa. In a medium bowl, combine the diced avocado, cucumber, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice. Gently toss to combine and season with salt to taste.
  7. Once the salmon is done baking, remove it from the oven. Drizzle the bang bang sauce over each fillet.
  8. Serve the bang bang salmon topped with a generous spoonful of avocado cucumber salsa. Enjoy!
