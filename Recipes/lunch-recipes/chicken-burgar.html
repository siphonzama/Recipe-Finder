chicken burgar

Ingredients

Buns:

  1. ½ cup (125ml) whole milk
  2. ½ cup plus 1 tablespoon (135ml) water
  3. 2 ½ teaspoons (9g) instant yeast
  4. 1 egg (48g)
  5. 1 egg yolk (18g)
  6. 3 ½ cups (525g) bread flour
  7. 1g amylase enzyme *optional for viewer*
  8. ⅓ cup (75g) granulated sugar
  9. 2 teaspoon (12g) fine sea salt
  10. 4 ½ tablespoons (63.7g) unsalted butter, softened
  11. Egg wash, 1 egg and 1 tablespoon of water whisked together

Chicken:

  1. 6 chicken thighs
  2. 3 tablespoon (40g) kosher salt
  3. 1 tablespoon (14g) sugar
  4. 2 teaspoon (9g) MSG
  5. Season and cure overnight

Breading and wash:

  1. 1 cup (240ml) milk
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 2 cups (300g) all-purpose flour
  4. 1 tablespoon (15g) kosher salt
  5. 2 teaspoons (9g) MSG
  6. 1 tablespoon (14g) powdered sugar
  7. 2 teaspoons (3g) fresh ground black pepper
  8. 1 ½ teaspoons (6g) ground white pepper
  9. 1 tablespoon (10g) garlic powder
  10. 1 tablespoon (14g) smoked paprika
  11. ½ teaspoon (2g) onion powder
  12. 1.5 teaspoon (4g) cayenne powder

Spicy Seasoning For Chicken:

  1. 4 teaspoons (8g) shichimi togarashi
  2. ½ teaspoon (1g) cayenne powder
  3. ½ teaspoon (1g) smoked paprika
  4. 1 teaspoons (5g) kosher salt
  5. 1 teaspoon (5g) sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon (2g) fresh ground black pepper

Pickles:

  1. ¼ cup (75g) kosher salt
  2. 2 cup (480ml) white vinegar
  3. ½ cup (120ml) water
  4. 1 cup (200g) white sugar
  5. 2 tablespoons (30g) mustard seed
  6. 1 teaspoon (5g) celery Seed
  7. 1 ½ teaspoons (>1g) turmeric powder
  8. 3 pickling cucumbers, cut in rounds

Mayo:

  1. 2 egg yolks
  2. 1 tablespoon (15g) lemon juice
  3. 1 teaspoon (7g) shiro dashi
  4. 2 teaspoons (14g) Dijon mustard
  5. 1 ½ cup (375ml) vegetable oil
  6. 1 tablespoon (15g) water
  7. 2 cloves garlic
  8. Pinch salt
  9. Pinch togarashi

Assembly:

  1. Buns
  2. Butter for toasting buns
  3. Pickles
  4. Chicken

Mayo for topping

  1. 6 slices pepper jack cheese
  2. 1 large tomato, sliced thinly
  3. 1 head Green leaf lettuce, leaves separated out
  4. Pickles for topping
  5. Mayo for topping
  6. Fries:
  7. Frozen Waffle Fries (Ore Ida)

INSTRUCTIONS:

Chicken:

Step 1

In a small mixing bowl combine salt, sugar, and msg Season chicken thighs generously with cure mixture on both sides, and place your chicken on a small tray or in an airtight container Cover chicken with plastic wrap or lid and let them cure in the fridge overnight.

Breading and Wash:

Step 2

In a medium bowl, combine all purpose flour, salt, msg, powdered sugar, black pepper, white pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika, onion powder, and cayenne powder. In a separate bowl crack and whisk 2 eggs. Once homogenized, whisk in 1 cup of milk and any remaining cure you might have. Lightly dust a small baking sheet with your flour dredge. Dunk a piece of chicken in the milk mixture, then drop into the flour dredge; pressing the flour into the chicken. Once that’s coated, shake off the excess flour and place it on your sheet. Repeat with the rest of your chicken. Spicy Seasoning For Chicken: In a small mixing bowl combine togarashi, cayenne, smoked paprika, salt, sugar, and fresh ground black pepper

Pickles:

Step 3

Combine all liquid ingredients in a medium saucepot and bring to a simmer. Turn off heat. Pack cucumbers in a glass jar and pour the hot liquid over. Cover and let cool.

Mayo:

Step 4

Add yolk, mustard, lemon juice, shiro dashi, water, and salt to a large jar or tall glass. Add oil to the top of the mixture. Set the immersion blender on the bottom of the vessel. Turn on the blender and gently pull toward the surface, the mixture will emulsify. Add grated garlic, and togarashi while blending. Use a splash of water to thin the mayo if it is too thick.

FRY:

Step 5

In a large heavy bottomed pot, add vegetable oil, and preheat to 350 F Carefully place the floured chicken in the hot oil, adding no more than 3 thighs at a time. Fry the chicken thighs for 6-8 minutes or until 165F internally. Remove the cooked thighs using a spider or strainer. Place the chicken on a wire rack to cool and drain. Immediately dust the cooked chicken thighs with the spicy chicken seasoning

Assembly:

SPICY CHICKEN SANDWICH:
Slice the bun in half horizontally with a sharp knife
In a large skillet over medium heat, add butter and toast both halves of the bun until they are golden brown, 1 ½ minutes.
Place the bottom bun on a plate and add sauce to the bun
Place 4 pickles on top of the sauce, and add the cooked chicken thigh
On the top bun add more sauce to cover to the edge of the toasted bun.
Place the bun on top of the cooked thigh and serve immediately
