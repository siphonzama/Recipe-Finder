Duration: 30 min

Equipment

Frying Pan

ingredients

  1. 4 slices Bread
  2. 1 cup Baked Beans Canned or Homemade
  3. 1 tbsp Oil
  4. 1 tbsp Butter
  5. 4 links Chicken Sausages
  6. 2 medium Tomatoes
  7. 8 no Mushroom
  8. 4 large Eggs
  9. Salt
  10. Black pepper
  11. 2 no Spring onions chopped

Method

step 1

Start by preparing the baked beans. You can use canned baked beans, But I prefer to make my own spice baked beans.

step 2

Slice your bread and toast them with little butter till crispy. Set aside.

step 3

a large frying pan. Add oil. Place chicken sausages, tomatoes and mushrooms. Cook these on medium to low heat till it is well seared and cooked completely. Keep tossing them around. Once they are charred to your liking, set them aside in the pan. Make room for frying some eggs.

step 4

Heat butter in the pan. Add in couple of eggs. Season with salt and pepper. Cook them as per your liking.
