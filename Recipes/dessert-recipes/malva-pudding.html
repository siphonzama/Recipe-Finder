malva pudding

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup (125ml) sugar
  2. 1 large egg
  3. 1 tablespoon (15ml) apricot jam
  4. 1 tablespoon (15ml) vinegar
  5. 1/2 cup (125ml) milk
  6. 1 1/4 cups (310 ml) cake flour
  7. 1 teaspoon (5ml) bicarbonate of soda
  8. Pinch of salt
  9. 2 tablespoons (30ml) butter, melted.

For the Sauce:

  1. 1 cup (250ml) fresh cream
  2. 1/2 cup (125ml) butter
  3. 1/2 cup (125ml) sugar
  4. 1/4 cup (60 ml) hot water
  5. 1 teaspoon (5ml) vanilla essence

Instructions:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease an ovenproof baking dish.

Step 2

In a bowl, beat the sugar and egg until light and creamy.

Step 3

Stir in the apricot jam and vinegar. In a separate bowl, sift the flour, bicarbonate of soda, and salt.

Step 4

Combine the milk and melted butter. Add the flour mixture to the egg mixture, alternating with the milk mixture, and mix well.

Step 5

Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish.

Step 6

Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 7

Prepare the Sauce:

While the pudding is baking, prepare the sauce. In a saucepan, combine the cream, butter, sugar, and hot water. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar and butter have melted. Bring to a simmer and cook for a few minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the vanilla essence.

Step 8

Assemble and Serve:

As soon as the pudding comes out of the oven, poke holes in it with a skewer. Pour the hot sauce evenly over the pudding.

Let it stand for a few minutes to absorb the sauce before serving. serve warm.

Tips:
For a richer flavor, you can use brown sugar in the sauce. Serve malva pudding warm with vanilla ice cream or custard.
